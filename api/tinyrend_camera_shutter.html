enum class tinyrend::camera::shutter::Type

Enumeration of shutter types.

Defines different types of camera shutters

Values:

enumerator GLOBAL

All pixels are exposed simultaneously (No rolling shutter)

enumerator ROLLING_TOP_TO_BOTTOM

Exposure starts from top and moves to bottom.

enumerator ROLLING_LEFT_TO_RIGHT

Exposure starts from left and moves to right.

enumerator ROLLING_BOTTOM_TO_TOP

Exposure starts from bottom and moves to top.

enumerator ROLLING_RIGHT_TO_LEFT

Exposure starts from right and moves to left.
